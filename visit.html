Location:

The museum is located at Torgallmenningen 8, Bergen City Centre

Admission:

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum. Children under 10 get free tours if they are with an adult. If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour. School administrations can contact us for free tours.

Accessibility:

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired. All signs explaining the exhibits are in both English and Norwegian. Live shows vary.

Food & Drink:

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
